
Pipeline to classifiy VAG signals

AUTOENCODE-KNEE: Automatic feature extraction using CNN-based Autoencoder from TimeFrequency Distribution of Knee Joint

PRE PROCESSING

1. Resample signal (8192)
2. Artefact elimination - double-cascaded moving average filter
3. signal normalisation - between -1 and 1
4. CEEMDAN EMD decomposition - 7 IMFs - use 3,4 and 5 plus the reconstructed signal
5. TFD for reconstructed VAG signals (89) and individual IMFs (3x89)

CNN Autoencoder

1. Load images via py torch data loader
2. transform (grayscale, tensors, normalisation)
3. Autoencoder to extract features (encoder part)
    compact feature embedding of the image - there are 4 images per signal
4. Aggregate the vectors to one (concatenate, average etc)
    this becomes the final vector (concatenation seems to make most sense)
